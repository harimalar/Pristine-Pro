Pristine Pro FAQ

Does Pristine Pro upload my photos or contacts?

No. Core scanning runs on-device.

Do you offer subscriptions?

No. Pristine Pro uses a one-time Lifetime Unlock model.

Can I review before deleting?

Yes. Manual review options are available across core cleaning flows.

How do I restore purchase?

Open Settings -> Purchase -> Restore Purchase.
