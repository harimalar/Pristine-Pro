Pristine Pro Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

1. Overview

Pristine Pro is a privacy-first utility app. Core scanning, analysis, and cleanup actions run on-device by default.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifiers
  • Cloud copies of private photos/videos/contacts/calendar data
  • Advertising identifiers for cross-app tracking

3. On-Device Processing

Duplicate detection, storage analysis, compression estimation, and cleanup recommendations are processed locally on your device.

4. Privacy Vault

Vault content is encrypted locally. Face ID/Touch ID is handled by Apple security frameworks; biometric templates are never accessible to the app.

5. Purchases

Lifetime unlock is processed by Apple via the App Store. Pristine Pro does not process payment card information directly.
